Classical Music

Over the years I have been obsessed with classical music, particularly classical piano works.

Who is my favorite composer?

Ludwig van Beethoven. My other favorite composers are Frédéric Chopin, Franz Liszt, Johann Sebastian Bach, Pyotr Ilyich Tchaikovsky, and more.

What are my favorite Beethoven works?

Check this melodic, lively, and magnificent piece: Piano Concerto No. 5 “Emperor”

this powerful piece: Symphony No. 5 in C minor “Fate”

and this passionate piano piece: Piano Sonata No. 23 “Appassionata”

What are my favorite Chopin pieces?

If I could listen to only three classical pieces for the rest of my life, what would they be?

  1. Beethoven, Piano Concerto No. 5 “Emperor”

This is my absolute favorite music piece ever.

  1. Chopin, Andante spianato et grande polonaise brillante in E-flat major, Op. 22

One of my favorite Chopin pieces.

  1. Chen Gang and He Zhanhao, Violin Concerto “The Butterfly Lovers”

This choice is particularly personal. “The Butterfly Lovers” is a Chinese theme written in the format of western orchestra music. The whole concerto is telling a tragic and moving story of two lovers in ancient China. This piece can always bring me peace of mind at any time.
